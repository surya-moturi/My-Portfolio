Project Details

📈 *Key Insights Uncovered*:

1. Gender Insights: Females dominate the buyer demographic, exhibiting higher purchasing power compared to males.

2. Age Group Dynamics: The 26-35 age group, predominantly female, constitutes the majority of buyers.

3. State-wise Sales: Uttar Pradesh leads in the number of orders, followed by Maharashtra and Karnataka in total sales amount.

4. Marital Status Trends: Married women have higher purchasing power and form a significant portion of the customer base.

5. Occupational Insights: Most buyers work in the IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors.

6. Product Category Preferences: The highest-selling products are from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.

🔍Married women aged 26-35 from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation, are most likely to purchase products from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.
